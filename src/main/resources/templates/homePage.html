Travel Allowance

WHAT IS TRAVEL ALLOWANCE?

If the employee chooses or is required to work in the office after 6:00 PM , the employer will provide an allowance to cover the costs of travel from work to home.

Enter Your Shift Preferences

HOW TO CALCULATE TRAVEL ALLOWANCE?

For each work day for an evening shift (from 6:00 PM), the employee will receive the allowance of 60 PLN.

Check Your Travel Allowance

IMPORTANT! The allowance will be paid with a month's delay. It will be sent to the employee's bank account with a monthly salary.
