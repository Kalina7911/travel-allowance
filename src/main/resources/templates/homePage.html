Travel Allowance

WHAT IS TRAVEL ALLOWANCE?

If the employee is required to work in the office after 6:00 PM , the employer will provide an allowance to cover the costs of travel from work to home.

WHAT IS THE AMOUNT OF THE ALLOWANCE?

For each day of work on the evening shift (at 6:00 PM), the employee will receive the allowance of 60 PLN.

IMPORTANT! The allowance will be paid with a month's delay. It will be sent to the employee's bank account with a monthly salary.
